Recipe 1 - Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (preferably unbleached and unbromated)
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Make sure you have a rack in the middle of the oven.
  2. Mix together the flour, eggs, sugar, and melted butter in a large bowl. Add the vanilla extract and mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased and floured 9x13 inch baking tin. Tap the tin gently to remove any air bubbles.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The cake should be golden brown on top and firm to the touch.
  5. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Once the cake is completely cool, you can frost it with your favorite frosting and decorate it as desired.

Prep Time:

30 minutes

Summary:

This recipe makes a delicious, moist, and flavorful cake. It is easy to make and requires minimal ingredients. The cake is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or just a sweet treat for yourself or your loved ones.
