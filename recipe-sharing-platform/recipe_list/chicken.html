Simple Grilled Chicken

Description:

This Simple Grilled Chicken recipe is quick and easy, perfect for a weeknight dinner or a summer BBQ. It’s flavorful, juicy, and a great source of protein!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, black pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours in the fridge).
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Prep Time:

10 minutes (plus marinating time)

Cook Time:

15 minutes

Serving Size:

Serves 4
